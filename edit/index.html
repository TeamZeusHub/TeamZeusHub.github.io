Electrical Team

The team that designs and prototypes energy storage solutions such as battery packs as well as test and program our data acquisition equipment.

Mechanical Team

The Mechanical Team works on a mini electric motorcycle prototype and fabricates the frame for the next generation electric motorcycle.

Business Team

Our budgeting and accounting team will be responsible for determining project costs, projecting our income, and keeping track of our expenditures; and the sponsorship team takes our proposal to corporations and grant providing institutions.

External Team

One pillar of our club is not only to enhance the reputation of Team ZEUS at a university level, but also the reputation of the University of Calgary in the international community.

Internal Team

Human resources, leadership development, safety programs as well as technical development are taken care of by the internal activities team.
